Questions & answers

What salary do you get when you work at Cooking.com?

Wage setting is based on current collective agreements, where criteria such as age and experience in the industry are important. In addition to this, there is the opportunity for a good salary development linked to the area of responsibility at the restaurant.

Anyone who wants to develop on Cooking.com and do a good job can take part in a good salary development. All employees at Cooking.com receive a salary increase every year.

Can you choose how many hours per week you work at us?

At Cooking.com you can work either full time or part time. A job at Cooking.com offers flexible working hours where you can combine a job at Cooking.com with studies and hobbies.

Many of our employees start working part-time with us, about two-thirds of the employees, and eventually you may decide to invest in Cooking.com full-time.

Cooking.com's history

The origins of today's Cooking.com originated in 2016 in San Bernadino, USA. Siblings Emma and Matthew Jones had an extreme interest in cooking since childhood and now wanted to open their own restaurant. But for this to be possible, the money was needed.

So instead of opening a restaurant, the siblings decided to open an online recipe page where they could share their interest with others. Today, Cooking.com has grown a lot and within a year or two we will also open up our first restaurant.
