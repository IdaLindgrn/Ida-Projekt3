Working at Cooking.com

Cooking.com offers flexible employment with good salary development

With us, you can work either full-time or part-time. We offer flexible working hours so that you can combine a job at Cooking.com with, for example, studies and hobbies.

At Cooking.com, we have a collective agreement with both HRF and the Union. The collective agreement regulates what salary you receive, where, among other things, age and experience are important. Anyone who wants to develop on Cooking.com and do a good job has a good salary development.
